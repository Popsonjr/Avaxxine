Avaxxine Mission At Work

Humanity

Avaxxine, born of a desire to mobilize the blockchain to provide financial assistance to anyone, anywhere, with a medical need! To prevent and alleviate human suffering wherever it may be found. Our purpose is to protect life and physical and mental health and ensure respect for the human being. We promote mutual understanding, friendship, cooperation and lasting peace among all peoples.

  • Mission Statement

    Avaxxine prevents and alleviates human suffering in the face of emergencies by mobilizing the power of crypto, the blockchain and investors. Together we will usher in a kind, generous and sympathetic future!

  • Vision Statement

    Avaxxine, through its strong network of donors and partners, will always there in times of need. We aspire to turn compassion into action so that all people affected by medical disasters around the world will have access to care, shelter and hope; our community is ready and prepared to help.

Avaxxine is a voluntary relief movement not prompted in any manner by desire for gain.
